About Us

HappyPupCare - we pride ourselves on being a team of dedicated and passionate pet lovers. Our primary goal is to provide exceptional services for your beloved furry friends. With years of experience, we understand the importance of creating a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable environment for pets while they are in our care. Our range of services is designed to cater to all aspects of your pet's well-being. For adventurous pups, we offer exhilarating off-leash hikes where they can explore nature, socialize with other dogs, and release their boundless energy under the supervision of our trained professionals. Training is another area of expertise for us. We believe in positive reinforcement methods that help shape your pet's behavior and strengthen the bond between you and your furry companion. Our trainers are skilled in addressing various obedience and behavioral issues, ensuring a well-mannered and happy pet. When you need to be away, rest assured that our hotel and daycare services will provide a home away from home for your pets.
